====== Beelzebul ======


===== Facts: =====


Beelzebul is the ruler of the demons and another name for Satan, or the devil.


    * Beelzebul is also spelled Beelzebub in some versions of the Bible.
    * It literally means "lord of flies" which means, "ruler over demons."
    * Since this is a name, it should be translated as a name.
    * It could also be translated as "Beelzebul the devil" to make it clear who is being referred to.
