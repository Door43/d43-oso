====== beg, beggar ======


===== Definition: =====


"To beg" is to ask for something urgently. Often it refers to asking for money.

    * A "beggar" is someone who regularly sits or stands in a public place to ask people for money.
    * Often people beg when they strongly need something, but don't know if the other person will give them what they ask for. This could also be translated as "plead" or "urgently ask."
    * When referring to asking for money, "beg" could also be translated as "demand money" or "regularly ask for money."
