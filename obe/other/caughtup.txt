====== caught up ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "caught up" refers to God transporting a person in a powerful, supernatural manner, usually to heaven.

  * The apostle Paul talked about being "caught up" to the third heaven. This could also be translated as "taken up."
  * Paul said that when Christ comes back, Christians will be "caught up" together to meet him in the air.
  * The phrase "caught up with" means having come up to someone after hurrying to reach him. A term with a similar meaning is "overtake" or "overtook."
